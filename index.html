In the lab

  • A new Bot Intelligence layer is coming online in the cockpit — five linked screens (performance, lineage map, regime matrix, idea pipeline, and exit lab) that turn forward-test data into a decision surface.
  • The Idea Pipeline screen tracks each strategy's lifecycle as a cohort funnel — experimental to paper to live-pilot to production — with per-bot readiness meters against an explicit promotion bar.
  • A cohort/promotion audit log now records every move between lifecycle stages, and a sources rail indexes where ideas come from — a research talent pool, TradingView signal emitters, and event/seasonality studies — with built-versus-untapped counts.
  • Regime output is being wired to gate strategy entries — favorable / neutral / caution / halt, with hysteresis so the label doesn't flap.
